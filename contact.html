Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment is necessary for most services. However, for medication reviews or health consultations, we recommend calling ahead to ensure a pharmacist is available to give you dedicated time.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer local delivery service in Birkirkara and surrounding areas for prescriptions and products. Contact us for details and to arrange delivery.

Can I get my prescription filled immediately?

In most cases, yes. We aim to fill prescriptions while you wait. For specialized medications, we may need a few hours or can have them ready for collection the next day.

Do you accept health insurance?

We work with most major health insurance providers in Malta. Please bring your insurance card and we'll process your claim directly where applicable.
