Over-the-Counter Medications

Expert guidance on non-prescription treatments

  • Sleep difficulties

Health Monitoring Services

Regular monitoring to help you manage your health conditions

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Regular blood pressure checks are essential for managing hypertension and preventing cardiovascular disease. Our pharmacists can measure your blood pressure and help you understand the results.

What we provide:
  • Accurate blood pressure measurements
  • Explanation of your readings
  • Lifestyle advice for blood pressure management
  • Referral to your doctor if needed

Blood Glucose Testing

For those managing diabetes or at risk of developing it, regular glucose monitoring is crucial. We offer convenient blood glucose testing and guidance on managing your levels.

What we provide:
  • Blood glucose level testing
  • Interpretation of results
  • Advice on diabetes management
  • Information on glucose monitoring devices
